Mercury

Mercury is the smallest planet in our solar system and the closest to the Sun.

Venus

Venus is the second planet from the Sun. It is named after the Roman goddess of love and beauty.

Earth

Earth is the third planet from the Sun and the only known planet to support life.

Mars

Mars is the fourth planet from the Sun and is often called the "Red Planet" due to its reddish appearance.

Jupiter

Jupiter is the largest planet in our solar system and is known for its Great Red Spot.

Saturn

Saturn is the sixth planet from the Sun and is famous for its spectacular ring system.

Uranus

Uranus is the seventh planet from the Sun and is unique because it rotates on its side.

Neptune

Neptune is the eighth and farthest planet from the Sun and is known for its deep blue color.
