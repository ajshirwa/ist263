Mercury

Mercury is a rocky planet with extreme temperature variations, ranging from freezing to scorching hot. It has no atmosphere to retain heat.

Venus

Venus is shrouded in thick clouds of sulfuric acid and is the hottest planet in the Solar System due to its runaway greenhouse effect.

Earth

Earth is a dynamic planet with a breathable atmosphere, liquid water, and diverse ecosystems supporting life.

Mars

Known as the Red Planet, Mars features towering volcanoes and vast canyon systems, as well as evidence of past water flow.

Jupiter

Jupiter's immense size and swirling storms, including the Great Red Spot, make it a standout in the Solar System.

Saturn

Famous for its intricate ring system, Saturn is a gas giant composed mostly of hydrogen and helium.

Uranus

Uranus rotates on its side and has a pale blue color due to methane in its atmosphere.

Neptune

Neptune is the windiest planet, with powerful storms and a deep blue color, also caused by methane.
