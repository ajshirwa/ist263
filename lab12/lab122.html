Planet Slideshow

Mercury

Mercury is the closest planet to the Sun and the smallest in the Solar System.

Venus

Venus is the second planet from the Sun and is similar in size and composition to Earth.

Earth

Earth is the third planet from the Sun and the only known planet to support life.

Mars

Mars is the fourth planet from the Sun and is often called the Red Planet.

Jupiter

Jupiter is the fifth planet from the Sun and the largest in the Solar System.

Saturn

Saturn is the sixth planet from the Sun, known for its iconic ring system.

Uranus

Uranus is the seventh planet from the Sun and has a unique sideways rotation.

Neptune

Neptune is the eighth planet from the Sun and is known for its intense blue color.
