Planet Slideshow

Mercury

Mercury is the smallest planet in our solar system and closest to the Sun.

Venus

Venus is the second planet from the Sun and is similar in structure to Earth.

Earth

Earth is the third planet from the Sun and the only known planet to support life.

Mars

Mars is known as the Red Planet and has the largest volcano in the solar system.

Jupiter

Jupiter is the largest planet in our solar system and has a massive storm called the Great Red Spot.

Saturn

Saturn is famous for its stunning ring system made of ice and rock.

Uranus

Uranus rotates on its side and is known for its bluish-green color.

Neptune

Neptune is the furthest planet from the Sun and has strong winds and storms.
